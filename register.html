Overload

Overload is designed to replace your paper workout journal. It combines the best features found in other apps with a minimalistic approach and a clean, easy-to-use interface. Give it a try. We know you'll love it!

Why?

We've been able to put together a great combination of simplicity, usability, and features. We believe that a workout log should do just what it says: log your workout

Strength

Being simple doesn't mean you have to compromise! overload has many features others don't have such as a summary page with stats from your previous workout, powerful graphing of both strength and cardio exercises, the ability to log supersets, cloud backup, export to Excel, and much more.

Personalization

Overload lets you create, print, track and chart workout routines on your own. By giving you access to powerful workout routines creation tools and a huge exercise database. Overload will enable you to take control of your workouts by letting you create effective workout routines

Inspiration

Track your progress and see yourself get strong. Overload keeps you moving towards your goals with features that let you enter your results, chart your progress, review your weight lifting history and more. Don't get stuck in a rut. Discover new routines, new equipment, and equipment free exercises. Your workouts never get boring.

Convenience

Overload works to keep you working out. Access your workout routines and enter results wherever you are from your smartphone, tablet or desktop. Schedule your workout on the go, enter your results, and see your successes. Let Overload guide you, inspire you, and help you get you healthy and strong.
